Hertfordshire Golf junior development
The county pathway — in plain English
Hertfordshire Golf runs one of England's most complete junior development programmes. Here's what exists and when your child should be considering each stage.
New to junior golf in Hertfordshire? Start at your child's club — every affiliated club has a junior section and most run beginner coaching. Once your child is ready for 9-hole or 18-hole competition, the pathway below is the natural next step.
Hertfordshire Golf programme
🏌️
Stage 2 · Ages 11–16
iTour
Stage 2
The perfect entry point for juniors who are ready to compete but don't yet have a handicap. No club membership required. Played on shorter courses to build confidence before the full 18-hole game. An Order of Merit runs throughout the season — best five scores count.
Find out more at Hertfordshire Golf →
Hertfordshire Golf programme
📋
Stage 3 · Handicap required
Futures Tour
Stage 3
The main competition structure for developing juniors with a handicap. Full 18-hole strokeplay and stableford events at clubs across Hertfordshire throughout the season. Order of Merit — best five scores count. The natural bridge between club golf and county championships.
Find out more at Hertfordshire Golf →

Stage 3–4 · Selected players
County Academy Programme
Stage 3 Stage 4
Extended playing experiences beyond club level. Coaching with Hertfordshire Golf's PGA professionals. Age-group specific sessions. Builds the bridge between club competition and county squad selection. Not open entry — players are identified through performance.
Find out more at Hertfordshire Golf →
Hertfordshire Golf programme
🏆
Stage 4–5 · Top performers
County Squads & Championships
Stage 4 Stage 5
The Boys County Championship (36-hole, HI 12.4 or lower) and Boys U16 Championship are the county's flagship events. The Boys Championship scratch winner qualifies for England Golf's Boys Champion of Champions at Woodhall Spa. County squad players also represent Hertfordshire in national inter-county competitions.

Girls golf in Hertfordshire
Hertfordshire Golf runs dedicated girls programmes alongside the main junior calendar. Here's what's available.

Girls Summer Series 2026

A dedicated summer competition series for girls across Hertfordshire. Order of Merit — best six scores count. Double points awarded for Girls Championships and 36-hole events. Open to girls who are members of an affiliated club and under 18 on 1 January 2026.

England Golf's national girls beginner programme, delivered through Hertfordshire affiliated clubs. No experience needed. Designed to give girls a fun, social first experience of golf.

Hertfordshire Junior Order of Merit
Points are awarded to the top finishers at every qualifying junior open across Hertfordshire. Best six scores count toward the final OOM standings.
How points work: Points are awarded at every qualifying Hertfordshire junior open — the more competitions your child enters, the more chances to accumulate points. Both gross and nett categories run in parallel. Double points at county championships and 36-hole events.
