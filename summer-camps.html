⭐ Jacob's Pick Scotland
St Andrews Golf Camp — The Home of Golf
📍 St Andrews, Scotland
Tournament Camp Ages 14–17 PGA coaching · Links Academy
A residential golf camp on the actual Home of Golf, welcoming junior golfers from over 38 countries. Campers stay at St Leonards (a private boarding school in the heart of town), get daily coaching from PGA professionals at the St Andrews Links Academy, and play across the St Andrews Links courses. Jacob attended the Tournament Camp.
Types of camp
Tournament Camp · Ages 14–17
Competition-focused week — Jacob's camp.
Development Camp · Ages 9–17
10 nights, all-round coaching for keen improvers.
Elite Camp · Ages 12–17
For handicaps 13 and under, with WAGR tournament access.
Parent/Child Camp · All ages
5-night luxury trip, tuition covers parent and junior together.
What every camper signs up to
Integrity
Honest scoring and good sportsmanship, on and off the course.
Respect
For the course, for opponents, and for yourself.
Resilience
Bouncing back from setbacks with a positive attitude.
Community
Looking out for campmates and embracing local culture.
Leadership
Taking responsibility for yourself, your kit and your group.
Kindness
Small, thoughtful gestures that make camp better for everyone.
"I had the time of my life here — the golf, the courses, the people I met. I'd recommend it to any junior golfer."
— Jacob, JuniorCaddie co-founder, Tournament Camp 2025
